About Union Church

We are called to be a reproducing church that is rooted in Christ, united in love, and devoted to establishing the Kingdom of God by proclaiming and demonstrating the gospel, making disciples, and planting churches throughout Riverside, the Inland Empire, the nation, and the ends of the earth.

I pray that they will all be one, just as you and I are one--as you are in me, Father, and I am in you. And may they be in us so that the world will believe you sent me.  -John 17:21

Jesus came and told his disciples, “I have been given all authority in heaven and on earth. Therefore, go and make disciples of all the nations," baptizing them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit. Teach these new disciples to obey all the commands I have given you. And be sure of this: I am with you always, even to the end of the age.”  -Matthew 28:18-20

The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, for he has anointed me to bring Good News to the poor. He has sent me to proclaim that captives will be released, that the blind will see, that the oppressed will be set free, and that the time of the Lord’s favor has come.  -Luke 4:18-19

But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes upon you. And you will be my witnesses, telling people about me everywhere - in Jerusalem,throughout Judea, in Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.  -Acts 1:8
